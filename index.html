1. Introduction

Key Launcher ("we", "our", or "the App") is developed by Lousify Tech. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

2. Information Collection and Use

Key Launcher is designed as a minimalist tool to help you access apps and contacts quickly. Most processing happens locally on your device.

A. Contacts Data (Sensitive Permission)
The App requests the android.permission.READ_CONTACTS permission. This is a core feature of the App. It allows the T9 keyboard to search for your contacts by name or phone number and enables quick actions like calling or messaging directly from the launcher. All contact data is processed locally. We DO NOT upload, sync, or transmit your contact information to any external servers.

B. Location Data
The App may request location access strictly to provide local weather information. This data is used only to fetch current weather and is not stored or shared.

3. Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Your data stays on your phone.
