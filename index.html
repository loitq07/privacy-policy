1. Introduction

Key Launcher ("we", "our", or "the App") is developed by Lousify Tech. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

2. Information Collection and Use

Key Launcher is designed as a minimalist tool to help you access apps and contacts quickly. Most processing happens locally on your device.

A. Contacts Data (Sensitive Permission)
The App requests the android.permission.READ_CONTACTS permission.

  • Purpose: This is a core feature of the App. It allows the T9 keyboard to search for your contacts by name or phone number and enables quick actions like calling or messaging directly from the launcher interface.
  • Handling: All contact data is processed locally on your device. We DO NOT upload, sync, or transmit your contact information to any external servers. Your data stays strictly on your phone.

B. Location Data
The App may request location access strictly to provide local weather information (temperature, AQI). This data is used only to fetch current weather for your area and is not stored or shared.

C. Personal Information
We do not collect personal identifiers like your name, email, or address unless you voluntarily provide them for support purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For App distribution and performance monitoring.
  • Google Play Billing: To process in-app purchases (Pro version).
  • Weather APIs: To provide real-time weather data.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. However, as 99% of your logic is local, your risks are minimized.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: loi.tranquang@gmail.com
